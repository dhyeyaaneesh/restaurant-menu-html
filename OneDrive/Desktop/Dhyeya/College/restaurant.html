Spaghetti All'Assassina

Spaghetti all'assassina (Italian for "assassin's spaghetti") is a spicy, pan-fried pasta dish from Bari, Italy, known for its unique method of cooking spaghetti directly in the sauce. This technique, called risottatura, is similar to cooking risotto and gives the pasta a distinct, caramelized, and crispy texture.


Price: $4.50



Smoked Mushroom and Bacon Pizza

A smoked mushroom and bacon pizza is a savory dish with a rich flavor profile created by the pairing of earthy mushrooms and smoky bacon. Recipes for this gourmet pizza can use either a tomato or creamy white garlic sauce base and often feature additional ingredients like caramelized onions and specialty cheeses.

Price: $5


Tiramisu

Tiramisu, from the Italian tiramisù, meaning "pick me up" or "cheer me up", is a classic Italian dessert with rich, creamy layers. It combines coffee-soaked ladyfingers (savoiardi) with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone cheese, and is topped with cocoa powder.

Price: $3.5
